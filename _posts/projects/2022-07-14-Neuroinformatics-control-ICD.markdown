ICD as reffered as Impulse Control Disorder is a neurological disorder that is characterized by a sudden onset of a sudden and severe change in the brain's electrical activity. The ICD is a common cause of seizures and other brain damage. In this project, I made an adaptive neural network that can be used to predict the ICD and it's early symptoms and amount of severity.
